About the Author

Abhishek graduated from NIT Patna and is a Software Developer by profession. He loves telling stories that spark imagination and bring out the hero inside every child. When not working, he enjoys exploring new places, painting, and reading with children. Through his stories, he shares a little magic with young readers everywhere.

Featured Book

Secret to Get Superpowers

Step into a magical world where every page holds a moral treasure! Follow little Miki and Mishi as they discover a hidden treehouse and meet Oru, a wise old owl with a diary full of 21 enchanting tales from all corners of India. Each story is set in a different Indian city, bringing alive its local traditions, festivals, and culture—while gently teaching timeless values like kindness, courage, patience, and friendship. This heartwarming collection is perfect for young readers and parents alike. Discover the real superpower hidden inside every child !
